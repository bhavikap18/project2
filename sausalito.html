About Oakland

Oakland is the eighth-most-populous city in California (417,870) being the largest in Alemda County. It is the home of the Oakland A's and Golden Sate Warriors. The Oracle Arena is famous for being the site of the Warriors playoffs and hosts conerts as well. The arena is the oldest of its kind in all of the NBA. The city serves as a tradin center for San Francisco. Its port is the largest in the SF bay. The city values its old Victorian homes as well as its diverse population. There is major Chinese population present in Oakland which was sparked by the Gold Rush during the 19th century. This led to the formation of Chinatown in Oakland. About San Jose

San Jose is a large city surrounded by hills in Silicon Valley, a major tech hub in California's Bay Area. The city has a plethora of diversity with a population of 1 million. It was primarily occupied by Spanish colonists. Many of the buildings in the city reflect the Spanish influence. Major global tech companies including Cisco Systems, eBay, Adobe Systems, PayPal, Brocade, Samsung, Acer, Hewlett Packard Enterprise, and Western Digital maintain their headquarters in San Jose, in the center of Silicon Valley. Downtown San Jose is famous for its lively night life especially during the holidays. Christmas in the park and their famous skating rink during the holiday season are major tourist attractions. Christmas in the park is a park full of Christmas trees decorated by a variety of people, most being kids in elementary school.
