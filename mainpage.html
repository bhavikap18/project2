About San Francisco

San Francisco was founded in June 29 1776 by colonists from Spain. SF is known for cool summers, steep hills and cool bay breeze. It is currently the head quarters of major economical companies. it is a city filled with culture and inclusive of all lifestyles; just >recently, during the summer, the city held a pride parade.
